Strawberry Dream Layer Cake

schedule

Prep Time

45 Min

groups

Yields

12 Slices

kitchen

Shelf Life

3 Days

shopping_basket Ingredients

restaurant_menu Method of Preparation

1

Prepare the Dry Ingredients

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). In a large, bubbly mixing bowl, whisk together the sifted flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Ensure they are well aerated for a fluffy sponge.

2

Cream Butter and Sugar

In a separate stand mixer, cream the room-temperature butter and granulated sugar on medium speed until light, fluffy, and pale in color (about 4-5 minutes). Scrape down the sides of the bowl as needed.

3

Fold and Bake

Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture in three additions, alternating with buttermilk. Pour the batter into two prepared 8-inch round cake pans and bake for 30-35 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.

Chef's Secret Tip

For an extra burst of flavor, macerate the fresh strawberries in a tablespoon of sugar and a dash of lemon juice for 15 minutes before layering them into the cake. It creates a natural syrup that soaks beautifully into the sponge!
